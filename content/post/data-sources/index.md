### General Macro and Regional Panel Data

*   [FRED, for everything!](https://research.stlouisfed.org/fred2/)
*   [BEA NIPA](http://www.bea.gov/iTable/iTable.cfm?ReqID=9)
*   [Penn World Tables](https://research.stlouisfed.org/fred2/categories/33402)
*   [BEA Regional Economic Accounts](http://www.bea.gov/regional/)
*   [American Community Survey from IPUMS](https://usa.ipums.org/usa/voliii/ACSsamp.shtml)
*   [General IPUMS](https://cps.ipums.org/cps/)
*   [Compustat/WRDS](http://wrds-web.wharton.upenn.edu)

### Intellectual Property and Consumer Panels

*   [Patent Data from the USPTO](http://www.uspto.gov/about-us/performance-and-planning/data-visualization-center)
*   [Trademarks from WIPO](http://ipstats.wipo.int/)
*   [Cross-country Historical Adoption of Technology (CHAT)](http://www.nber.org/data/chat/)
*   [Nielsen Consumer Panel](http://research.chicagobooth.edu/nielsen/datasets/consumer-panel-data.aspx)

### Manufacturing and Industrial Statistics

*   [NBER-CES Manufacturing Industry Database](http://www.nber.org/data/nberces5809.html)
*   [Census Industry Concentration Ratios](https://www.census.gov/econ/concentration.html)
*   [UNIDO Industrial Statistic Database](http://www.unido.org/index.php?id=1002104)
*   [Survey of Management Across Sector(WMS)](http://worldmanagementsurvey.org/?page_id=183)
*   [Census-BDS Firm Characteristics](http://www.census.gov/ces/dataproducts/bds/data_firm.html)
*   [Census-BDS Establishment Characteristics](http://www.census.gov/ces/dataproducts/bds/data_estab.html)
*   [Synthetic Longitudinal Business Database (SynLBD)](http://www.census.gov/ces/dataproducts/synlbd/)
